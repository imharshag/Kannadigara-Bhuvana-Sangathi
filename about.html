SOMAIHA MALEYUR
-EDITOR, FOUNDER

Somaiha Maleyur, the esteemed editor and founder of Bhuvana Sangathi, hails from the humble village of Maleyur in the Chamarajanagar district. With a deep passion for journalism and a commitment to bringing truth to the forefront, Somaiha has dedicated his life to political reporting. His journey from a small village to the helm of a renowned political monthly newspaper is a testament to his perseverance and vision. Under his leadership, Bhuvana Sangathi has grown into a respected publication, known for its insightful analysis and unwavering dedication to journalistic integrity. Somaiha's roots in Maleyur have instilled in him a profound understanding of grassroots issues, which continues to influence the newspaper's commitment to covering stories that matter to the people.
