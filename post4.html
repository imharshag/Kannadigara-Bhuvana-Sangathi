Bhuvana Sangathi fecilitation

Bhuvana Sangathi recently celebrated a significant milestone with the 15th anniversary of our successful publication. The event was a grand occasion, highlighted by the esteemed presence of our honorable minister, Shrinivas Prasad, who felicitated the gathering. This special event not only marked our enduring commitment to community welfare and development but also served as a platform for introducing new advancements and recognizing the contributions of our dedicated team.

Minister Shrinivas Prasad greeted us warmly and delivered an inspiring speech to the youth assembled at the ceremony. In his address, he commended Bhuvana Sangathi for its unwavering dedication and consistent efforts over the past 15 years. He highlighted the crucial role of the press in shaping public opinion and fostering a well-informed society. His words resonated deeply with the audience, particularly the young attendees, motivating them to actively participate in community initiatives and strive for excellence.

In addition to the felicitation, the event also featured the introduction of our newly formed tech team, which is poised to drive Bhuvana Sangathi into the digital future. The tech team was introduced to the audience, showcasing their innovative projects and plans to enhance our publication's reach and impact through modern technology. This introduction signifies a new chapter for Bhuvana Sangathi, emphasizing our commitment to embracing technological advancements to better serve our community.
